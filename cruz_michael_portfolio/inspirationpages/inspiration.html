Artist Inspiration

Kim Jung Gi

Kim Jung Gi is a Graphic Illustrator that is best known for his technical drawings and not using any reference to draw any kind of figures. He is my inspiration due to the his methods of drawing and having his way of imagining all of his drawings come to life by having it look more realistic. I have always aspired to draw like him, and to implement it in my everyday life.

Kim Jung Ji

Saul Bass

Saul bass is a Graphic Designer that makes movie posters such as movies from the 1970s to the early 90s. He is one of my inspirations due to his art style and how he utilizes geometric shapes and expressing his emotions and movies through that. Lastly, Saul Bass' work ethic and how much he puts out media and new graphic posters has been inspiring, and I always aspire to put out any kind of content that I could be proud of.

Saul Bass

Keith Haring

Keith Haring is a graphic designer and an Illustrator that is based in New York, and his art pieces are mostly social commentary about various topics, and is best known for their figure drawings. I really like how simple yet so effective his drawings are, and it even reached to pop culture and has the influence around the world, working with artists and even working with well known companies.

Keith Haring
